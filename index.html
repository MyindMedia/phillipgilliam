About the Author

Born in Norfolk, Virginia, Phillip Gilliam graduated from Morgan State University with a degree in Physics and Engineering before spending decades as a communications engineer at Verizon. He holds two United States patents in Biomechanics.

But it was his love of film noir that truly defined him. Mesmerized by stylish Hollywood crime dramas and hard-boiled characters‚ÄîPhilip Marlowe, Jake Gittes, Vito Corleone‚Äîhe found himself listening to Sinatra, Ellington, and Billie Holiday before age fifteen.

After studying the actual 1888 Jack the Ripper crimes in grisly detail, he walked the alleyways of Baltimore's Fells Point at night until he found the perfect locations to set his tale. The John Talion Mysteries were born from this passion.
